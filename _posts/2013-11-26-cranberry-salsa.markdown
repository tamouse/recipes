yet another cranberry sauce alternative

Chop 2 pounds cranberries, 4 apples, 4 oranges, 2 bunches green
onions, 2 Serrano chiles, the juice of 1 lime, a bit of salt, and 1/3
to 1/2 cup sugar. Let flavors blend.
