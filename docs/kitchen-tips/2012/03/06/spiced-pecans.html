Spiced Pecans 06-Mar-2012

Categories: kitchen-tips Tags: pecans garnishes

Makes an interesting garnish, listed in Roasted Pumpkin Soup

Ingredients

(:comment bulleted list of ingredients, with notes if necessary:)

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 teaspoon (kosher) salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup pecans

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 350&degF;.
  2. Line a baking sheet with foil.
  3. In small bowl, combine melted butter, salt, and cayenne.
  4. Add pecans, toss to coat well.
  5. Spread evenly on foil.
  6. Bake 5 minutes or until toasted, watching closely to ensure pecans do not burn.
  7. Cool completely.
