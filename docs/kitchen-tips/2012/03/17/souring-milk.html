Souring Milk 17-Mar-2012

Categories: kitchen-tips Tags:

An alternative to using buttermilk.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of sweet milk
  • 1 1/3 tablespoons white vinegar

Directions

  1. Add the vinegar to the milk and set aside for 15 minutes before using.
