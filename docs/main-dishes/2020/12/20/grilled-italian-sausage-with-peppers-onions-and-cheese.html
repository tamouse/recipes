Grilled Italian Sausage with peppers, onions, and cheese 20-Dec-2020

Categories: main-dishes Tags: italian-sausage sausage onions peppers cheese vegan-cheese grilled gluten-free dairy-free

I found this way to make these deliious italian sausages on tiktok; I can’t remember the creator, but she has a lot of great recipes, tips, and ideas.

Ingredients

  • 5 italian sausages, split length-wise
  • 8 ounces frozen mixed peppers and onions for stir fry, or cut fresh, thawed
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 8 ounces dairy-free mozzarella shreds

Direcctions

  1. brown sausages in skillet
  2. add pepers and onions, saute until soft
  3. preheat broiler to High
  4. line baking sheet with foil
  5. place sausages cut-side-up on foil lined bakind sheet
  6. smother with onions and peppers
  7. cover with mozzarella
  8. broil on high until cheese sort of “melts” – vegan cheese doesn’t really melt like regular moz, so pay attention so it does’t burn!
