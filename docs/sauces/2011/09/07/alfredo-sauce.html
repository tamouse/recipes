Alfredo Sauce 07-Sep-2011

Categories: sauces Tags: parmesean-cheese italian-cuisine

A creamy, cheesy sauce to go over pasta or other things.

This starts with your basic white sauce and adds parmesan. Couldnâ€™t be easier, really!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white sauce
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Make the white sauce according to the directions

  2. Before the sauce thickens too much, add the parmesan cheese. Stir until desired thickness/texture emerges.
