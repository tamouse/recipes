Gluten-free no-knead bread

| baked-goods | flax-meal | sorghum-flour | potato-starch | rice-flour | tapioca-starch | xanthan-gum | yeast | gluten-free |

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup flax meal
  • ¾ cup hot water
  • 3 cups sorghum flour
  • 1½ cups potato starch (not potato flour)
  • 1 cup sweet rice flour
  • ½ cup tapioca starch/flour
  • 4 teaspoons xanthan gum
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 3½ teaspoons active yeast
  • 2 cups + 1 tablespoon warm water, divided
  • ¼ cup honey
  • Olive oil, to brush top, optional
